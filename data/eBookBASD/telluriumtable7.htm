References and Notes for Tellurium ( Te )


      E74       K. B. S. Eriksson, J. Opt. Soc. Am. 64, 1272 (1974). 

      HM64      M. B. Handrup and J. E. Mack, Physica 30, 1245 (1964). Energy level values have been reduced by 0.47 cm-1, as suggested by [E74].

      M00       D. C. Morton, Astrophys. J. Suppl. Ser. 130, 403 (2000). 

      M58       C. E. Moore, Atomic Energy Levels, Natl. Bur. Stand. (U.S.) Circ. 467. Vol. III (1958); reprinted as Natl. Stand. Ref. Data Ser., Natl. Bur. Stand. (U.S.) 35 (1971). 

      MCS75     W. F. Meggers, C. H. Corliss, and B. F. Scribner, Natl. Bur. Stand. (U.S.), Monogr. 145 (1975). 

      MV75      C. Morillon and J. Vergés, Phys. Scr. 12, 129 (1975). Wavelengths for Te I shorter than 9800 Å are calculated from energy levels given in this reference.
